What people are saying . . .

Since 1966, the Hagadone Corporation has had a strong presence in the Hawaiian Islands. As owner of the State’s largest circulation magazine, This Week, and a number of other publications like the Waikiki Beach Press, over the last six decades, the Hagadone’s have left their mark on the media and publishing industries in Hawaii.  The Hagadone Corporation also owned KTOH radio on Kauai and the Garden Island News at one time on the company’s history.
